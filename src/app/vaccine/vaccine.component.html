Coronavirus (Covid-19) is disease that causes the

How does the disease spread?

The virus that causes Covid-19 disease is transmitted
mainly through droplets that an infected person
secretes when they cough, sneeze or breathe. The weight of
these droplet is too heavy to remain suspended in the air,
so they quickly fall to the floor or surfaces.
